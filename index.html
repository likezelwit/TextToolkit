What is Text Converter?
Text Converter is a web application that provides various tools to transform and analyze text in different ways. From converting text to numbers to analyzing text statistics, our tools help you manipulate text for various purposes.
Is this service free to use?
Yes, our basic service is free to use with a token system. Free users get 50 tokens that reset every 3 hours, while logged-in users get 100 tokens that reset every 2.5 hours. Each text conversion consumes one token.
Can I use this on mobile devices?
Absolutely! Our website is fully responsive and works perfectly on all devices including smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. The interface automatically adjusts to provide the best experience on your device.
What is Text to Number conversion?
Text to Number conversion is a process that transforms alphabetic characters into numbers based on their visual similarity. For example, 'E' becomes '3', 'O' becomes '0', and 'A' becomes '4'. This is often used in leet speak and for creating unique numeric representations of text.
How does Language G work?
Language G is a specialized language processing tool that focuses on Indonesian language features. It can help with translation, transliteration, and various text processing tasks specific to Indonesian language structure and grammar.
What is Dry Text conversion?
Dry Text conversion removes most vowels from text while keeping only the first vowel in each word. This creates a more condensed version of your text that's still readable but uses fewer characters. It's useful for creating unique usernames, short messages, or just for fun text transformations.
How can Long Text tool help me?
The Long Text tool is designed to help you process and analyze extensive documents. It provides features for formatting, summarizing, and extracting key information from large amounts of text. It's perfect for students, researchers, or anyone working with lengthy documents.
What makes Monkey Text special?
Monkey Text adds playful modifications to your text, creating a fun and unique style. It can randomize capitalization, add special characters, or apply other creative transformations to make your text stand out. It's great for social media posts, creative writing, or just adding some fun to your messages.
What can I do with CatText?
CatText transforms your regular text into adorable cat-style messages with playful modifications. It adds cat-like expressions, purrfect formatting, and feline-themed elements to make your text more fun and expressive. It's perfect for cat lovers or anyone wanting to add a cute touch to their messages.
How does AlienText work?
AlienText converts your regular text into an otherworldly language using unique symbols, special characters, and extraterrestrial formatting. It creates mysterious messages that appear to be from another planet, perfect for sci-fi themed content, creative writing, or just having fun with friends.
What makes HacKerTEXT unique?
HacKerTEXT transforms your text into a hacker-style format with alternating capitalization, special character substitutions, and code-like elements. It creates that distinctive cyberpunk aesthetic often seen in movies and games, perfect for creating tech-themed content or just for fun.
What can I do with Statistik & Analisis Teks?
Statistik & Analisis Teks provides detailed statistics about your text including character count (with and without spaces), word count, sentence count, paragraph count, estimated reading time, and most frequently used words. It's perfect for writers, students, and anyone who needs to analyze text for various purposes.
How do I create an account?
To create an account, click on the Login button in the top right corner of the page, then select the option to create a new account. Fill in your details and submit the form. Once registered, you'll have access to additional features and more tokens.
What are the benefits of having an account?
Having an account gives you several benefits: more tokens (100 instead of 50), faster token reset time (2.5 hours instead of 3 hours), ability to save your conversion history, and access to premium features in the future.
How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, click on the Login button and then select the "Forgot Password" option. Enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions on how to reset your password.
What are tokens and how do they work?
Tokens are credits that you use to access our text conversion tools. Each time you use a tool to convert text, one token is deducted from your balance. Free users get 50 tokens that reset every 3 hours, while logged-in users get 100 tokens that reset every 2.5 hours.
How can I check my token balance?
Your current token balance is displayed in the top navigation bar next to the theme toggle button. It shows your current token count and the time remaining until your tokens reset.
When do my tokens reset?
For free users, tokens reset every 3 hours. For logged-in users, tokens reset every 2.5 hours. The countdown timer shows how much time is left until your next reset.
Can I get more tokens without waiting for the reset?
Currently, the only way to get more tokens is to wait for the reset or to create an account, which gives you more tokens and a faster reset time. In the future, we may offer premium plans with additional tokens.
